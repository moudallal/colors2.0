Let Me Introduce My Self

Hello there, I'm Mohamad Moudallal, and I'm a 17 years old teenager born in the 00's in Beirut, Lebanon. I'm mostly known for my Rubik's Cube skills and as the official Lebanese National Record holder with a 11.29 seconds solve. I'm a guy who's interested in Computers and Science mostly. My love for computers started since I was just 3 years old when my father bought me a laptop so I can play video games. Through the years, I started scratching the surface of programming. I first met hacking because hacking other's Facebook account was "cool" according to my age. I actually ended up phishing some of my friends' and family's Facebook accounts and I guess that was it. In 2010, I met Adobe Illustrator which was my debut in design. I started designing some beginner drawings and wallpapers. In 2011, I met an organization called "The Little Engineer". I sarted with one of their courses about robotics, and that's when I met programming. I discovered it's real power and how you could make a robot do whatever you want. After that, I started an easy programming language called Python that includes some simple math problem solving. Later on, I started learning web development, mainly using HTML, CSS and Javascript. I'll soon be uploading the first webpage I ever designed that I'm so proud of, especially to see where I have reached right now after all these years. In 2014, my interests changed a bit, I was interested in iOS App development and that's when I started learning Swift, Apple's new programming language. To be honest, iOS development was lots of fun, but there were too many updates to Swift, just too many, and it was overwhelming. After 2 years of iOS App development, I went back to the web, and got myself more comfortable with UI and UX design which boosted my opportunities for work. My first portfolio website that got online was back in Summer of 2016, you can check it out in the link inside my portfolio section. Thanks to this website that I designed by myself, I got a request to work as a Pixel Perfectness Officer (It's actually a Front-End Developer + UI/UX Designer) for Zoomaal. That was the first fixed job I ever got, specifically on the age of 16. Note that the first job I got was a logo designer for an entrepreneurship here in Beirut. I worked for Zoomaal Inc. for about 6 months while I was designing their new fundraising platform, Givingloop, as part of the Zoomaal Team. On February 2017, I got out of Zoomaal and went back to being a freelancer where I got a project to develop TeensWhoCode's Summer Camp Website on May 2017. This project was fun to build and finish. Meanwhile, I'm currently co-founding a new team called Polyvoid with my partner Firas Koussa to develop our personal projects plus do some freelance work, build and design websites for other clients. Apart from my career, I started playing guitar on November 2016, self-taught online, like all my other skills. Right now I spend my free time learning new songs and composing some of my own, teaching my self the piano, cubing, eating burgers, and hanging out with my friend in the lovely Beirut. At last, I want to say that I'm never done learning, I always strive for more. I also want to thank my family, friends and colleagues for supporting me through my journey.
Now you know well about me, friends?
